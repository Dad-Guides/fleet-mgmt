## 19. Ultrasonic Cleaner — Vevor 6L ✅ On Hand

> Confirmed in bike house. A major shop asset — dramatically reduces cleaning time on metal drivetrain components.

### What to Clean in the Ultrasonic
| Component | Bikes | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Cassettes and freewheels | All bikes | Remove from wheel first |
| Chainrings | B1, B3, B4 | Remove from crank before cleaning |
| Derailleurs (front and rear) | All bikes | Remove from bike; submerge fully |
| Jockey wheels | All bikes | Remove from rear derailleur cage |
| Chain | All bikes | Use mesh basket; replaces scrubber for deep cleans |
| Brake calipers (rim only) | B1, B4 | Remove pads first |
| Crankset arms | B1, B3, B4 | Alloy only — no carbon |
| Brake levers (alloy) | B1, B4 | Remove from bars; inspect pivot after |
| Pedal bodies (alloy) | All bikes | Remove axle/bearings first |
| Small bolts and hardware | All bikes | Use the parts basket |
| Hubs (shells only) | All bikes | Remove bearings/cones first |

### ⚠️ Do NOT Put in the Ultrasonic
| Item | Reason |
|------|--------|
| Sealed cartridge bearings | Cavitation strips grease through seals |
| Carbon fibre components | Delamination risk |
| Rubber seals, O-rings, foam rings | Ultrasonic destroys rubber compounds |
| Painted or decal-covered parts | Paint and decals will lift |
| Electronic components | B6 Concord display, motor connectors |
| Brake pads (any type) | Contamination — discard and replace |
| Plastic components | Degrades with heat and cavitation |

### Recommended Settings & Solutions
| Setting | Value | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Temperature | 50–60°C | Optimal for grease emulsification |
| Cycle time | 10–20 min | Heavily soiled parts may need two cycles |
| Solution (general) | Simple Green 1:10 in water | Safe, biodegradable, effective |
| Solution (heavy grease) | Zep Industrial Purple 1:5 | Caked-on drivetrain grease |
| Rinse | Clean water immediately after | Prevent residue and flash rust on steel |
| Dry | Compressed air or low heat gun | Dry thoroughly before re-lubing |
| Re-lube | Always | Ultrasonic strips all lubrication |
